This piece examines the seasonal heat protection regulations implemented annually across Gulf Cooperation Council nations. Beginning June 15 in the UAE, these midday work ban rules restrict outdoor labor during peak temperature hours to safeguard workers from extreme heat exposure, and the post provides guidance for employers operating across multiple GCC jurisdictions.
