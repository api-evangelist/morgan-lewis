This post covers three major developments affecting government contractors: the House Armed Services Committee's draft FY27 National Defense Authorization Act with focus on acquisition reform, the DOJ's new expedited review process for False Claims Act whistleblower cases with 60-120 day initial assessments, and House approval of the BUILD America 250 Act authorizing approximately $580 billion for surface transportation infrastructure from 2027-2031.
